Have operational queries regarding technical window dimensions, custom fabric durability, order factory synchronization, or automation configurations? Browse through our verified architect logs.

1. What are the pricing calculation models for custom window blinds?
The actual cost of our custom blinds depends on the square footage of your window structure (Width x Height) and your chosen fabric catalog variant (such as standard translucent, thermal protection weave, or heavy-duty solid blackout layers). Our design specialists will provide a fully transparent, detailed estimation customized for your space during your doorstep evaluation session.
2. Do you offer free physical measurements and doorstep consultation?
Yes, absolutely! Our engineering team conducts doorstep site visits across Delhi, Gurgaon, and Noida with zero consultation fees and zero service charges. Our experts utilize professional laser-measurement devices to ensure absolute accuracy with zero-millimeter deviation errors. Additionally, you can inspect real fabric catalogs and premium shade swatches in person during the visit.
3. What is the standard duration required from measurement to final installation?
Once your finalized profile dimensions and fabric configurations are selected, the order details are immediately synced with our modern factory manufacturing department for automated custom processing. For all standard blind profiles, production and perfect deployment setup are seamlessly executed within 3 to 5 operational working days.
4. What type of onsite cover warranty do you supply on mechanism gears?
Serina Blinds provides a comprehensive 1 to 3-year absolute onsite component replacement warranty covering all friction elements, heavy-duty roller control clutches, metallic mounting brackets, and automated silent motors. If any component fails to operate smoothly during the deployment cycle, our support technician will visit your site to deliver immediate rectifying assistance at no extra cost.
5. Are these window fabrics dust-repellent and safe for home installation?
Yes, our premium polyester weaves and glassfiber blind structures are micro-engineered with advanced anti-static coatings that actively prevent dust, moisture, or smoke particles from settling on the surfaces. Maintenance is incredibly simple—you can maintain their pristine condition and extend their lifecycle easily by giving the linear surfaces a smooth wipe-down using a damp microfiber cloth.
6. Can we upgrade these custom blinds into Alexa or smartphone control automation?
All of our custom Roller Blinds and Zebra Blinds profiles are fully upgradable with advanced automated silent gear tracking controllers. We implement premium smart-integrated motors that can be managed seamlessly via multi-channel RF remotes, dedicated iOS/Android smartphone applications, or smart home ecosystems including Google Assistant and Amazon Alexa voice commands.
